How to get your AniList credentials

1
Visit AniList Developer
Go to https://anilist.co/settings/developer and log in to your AniList account
2
Create New App
Click "Create New Client" and fill in the app name (e.g., "My AniSync Extension")
3
Set Redirect URL
Set the redirect URL exactly as shown in the form below (format: https://[extension-id].chromiumapp.org/)
4
Copy Credentials
After creating, copy your Client ID and Client Secret to the form on the right
5
Save & Continue
Click "Save Configuration" to complete the setup
